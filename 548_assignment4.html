A4: Making a piece of furniture

Initial idea and sketches

For this assignement, we had to make a piece of furniture and I decided to make a bedside table for the incredibly narrow space between my side of the bed and the wall.

Once I figured out the measurements, I drew my parts in Rhino using only the top view. I decided to go with press fit joints and made holes in the side panels that were the same width as my plywood board's thickness (~ 0.39 ").

Milling the pieces!
